FAQ
What is a domain name?
A domain name is like an address for your website. It’s what people type into their browser to find you, like www.example.com.
Why do I need a domain name for my website?
A domain name makes it easy for people to find your website and gives it a professional look. It’s also easier to remember than a long or random web link.
What’s the difference between a domain name and a website?
A domain name is your address, and your website is the house at that address. You need both to have an online presence.
